Nishita

A Photography Wordpress Theme.

  1. Installation

    • Upload the downloaded theme into your Wordpress themes folder - "wp-content/themes".
    • Create Archives/Gallery: Create a New Page and Select Archives Template under Page Template.

  2. How to upload Photos

    • Upload the photo (both full size and thumbnail versions). Full size images should be 720px in width and thumbnails should be 75px in width.
    • Paste or Insert into Post the full size image in the post body field and the URL of the thumbnail in the Excerpt field. Write in the title and chose a category and post.
    • If you want to add a description, you can do it under the picture in the post body field.
